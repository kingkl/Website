Services

In addition to normal cleaning and fillings we offer a wide variety of dentures and tooth repair services.

Complete Dentures
We offer both conventional dentures, where you have to wait for the gums to heal, and immediate dentures that can be placed right away.
Bridges
Gaps caused by missing teeth can be filled by placing crowns on the teeth on either side of the gap and cementing the bridge permanently into place.
Partial Dentures
Similar to bridges but removable. Clasps attached to the crowns next to the gap hold it in place.
Dental Implants
We also have dental implants that can be used to make your dentures permanent.

For more information visit Dental Health Online.
