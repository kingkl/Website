Trash to Treasure

At Manholes Can Be Magic, the idea that one person’s trash is another’s treasure can be taken one step further. Many people find that attractive and unusual art can make good conversation pieces and fun decorations for their homes.

We offer products based on manhole covers and in-ground utility access covers from the U.S. and around the world. Under the Manholes hyperlink in the upper right of the page you can find a list of the manhole images we have available.

We try our best to find products that are fun and interesting. At the moment we have three products we hope you will enjoy.


Prints  

We offer prints of all of our manholes and access utility covers in 10" X 10", 15" X 15" and 20" X 20" sizes.

T-Shirts  

Our t-shirts are unique and unusual. Off-white in color and 100% cotton. Pre-washed to prevent shrinking. Available in Small, Medium, Large, and X-Large.

Rugs  

Our rugs make interesting accents indoors and outdoors. They also make unique tapestries when hung on walls. Available in 26" and 36" widths.

History

Manholes have a long and rich history having been around since ancient Rome at the very least. To learn more about manholes and their history you can visit Wikipedia.org and Sewerhistory.org.

Manholes as a Hobby

Known as "Drain Spotting" or "Drainspotting", photographing manholes is a hobby that anyone can take part in. Manholes and utility access covers are everywhere and anyone who pays attention to their environment can see the work and attention to detail that goes into them. Some manholes can be quite beautiful.

On Amazon.com you can find books on manholes and drainspotting like "Manhole Covers" by Mimi and Robert A. Melnick and "Drainspotting: Japanese Manhole Covers" by Rome Camerota. On the internet you can also find websites on drainspotting and manhole photography where you can learn more about the hobby. On some of these sites you can even upload your own photos.
