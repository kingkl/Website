About Us

Welcome to Manholes Can Be Magic. We are a new web based company founded in 2014 that offers products based on images of manholes and utility access covers from around the world. We are just getting started and hope you enjoy your visit. Below you can meet the people behind our company and you will find a video about the manhole images we have available.


Sylvia

Originally from Austria, Sylvia is a world traveller who enjoys the hobby of photographing manholes and in-ground utility access covers. Over the years she has amassed a large library of images. This site shows only a small selection of her collection.

Beverley

Beverley has travelled with Sylvia a great deal and has observed her hobby and saw its potential. She is a great idea person and came up with the idea for this site.

Kandice

Kandice is a second year student at Gwinnett Technical College Studying Web Site Design. The design and creation of this site is hers. This website was originally her final project for CIST 1510 - Web Development I. She has recreated this website using Bootstrap.
