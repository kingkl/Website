About Us

Strandt & Tappen, Inc. is a family owned business located in beautiful Lyons, Wisconsin. The company has been manufacturing top quality wagons and carriages of all kinds since 1905. Visit us and we will be happy to give you a tour. Be sure to visit our museum. We have an informative selection of wagons, carriages and images on display.
