History

In the past wagons allowed people to travel long distances while carrying supplies and possessions. Because of this, wagons have had an important impact on history. You can visit the Wikipedia page on wagons to learn more. Be sure to visit our company museum. We have a wide selection of the wagons and carriages from the life of Strandt & Tappen, Inc. and before the company existed.
